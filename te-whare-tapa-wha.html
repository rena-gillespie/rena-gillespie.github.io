Te Whare Tapa Whā

Well Being Plan

My plan to use the Te Whare Tapa Whā model

Taha Tinana(Physical Wellbeing) = Going on an hour long walk twice a week. Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) = Eat dinner with my family and also cook for them every now and then. Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) = Spend some time knitting in my down time. Tidy my desk to get rid of clutter. Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) = Write down good things that happened today. Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) = Stand outside in my garden and listen to the birds.
