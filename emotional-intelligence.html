Today we researched "Emotional Intellegence" or "EQ" and it's value.

EQ is the ability to read people's emotions, management of your own emotions and reading the vibe of the room.

This is important with relationships, like seeing your friend is upset and offering to console them or listening to their worries.

Or identifying your own emotions and managing them, like noticing you're frustrated with a project and making the choice to step back, breath, and go for a walk. Or cherishing a moment when you feel clam and happy.
